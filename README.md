## ðŸ“ˆ Key Insights & Results

### 1. **Massive Employment Shift from Agriculture to Services**
- Agriculture employment declined from 45% (1991) to 26% (2022) globally
- Service sector grew to dominate at 52% of global employment
- Implication: Countries need reskilling programs for displaced agricultural workers

### 2. **Regional Unemployment Disparities**
- Middle East & North Africa: Highest unemployment at 11.2%
- East Asia: Lowest at 4.1%
- Youth unemployment 2-3x higher than adult unemployment in all regions

### 3. **Extreme Global Economic Inequality**
- Top 10 countries: Average GDP $18.5 trillion
- Bottom 10 countries: Average GDP $5.2 billion (3,500x difference)
- Gap has widened 23% since 1991 despite development initiatives

### 4. **Predictable Trends**
- Industry employment follows predictable automation curve
- Unemployment spikes correlate with major economic crises (2008, 2020)
- GDP growth rate slowing in mature economies, accelerating in emerging markets
